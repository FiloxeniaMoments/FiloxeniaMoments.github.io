Wedding Planning Services

Creating your perfect day

Full-Service Wedding Planning

Our comprehensive wedding planning package covers every aspect of your special day from conception to execution. We'll be by your side throughout the entire planning process, handling all logistics, vendor coordination, budget management, and day-of execution.

  • Unlimited planning consultations
  • Venue selection and management
  • Vendor recommendations, negotiations, and management
  • Budget creation and management
  • Design concept and implementation
  • Complete timeline planning
  • Full day-of coordination (up to 12 hours)
  • Rehearsal coordination

Partial Wedding Planning

Perfect for couples who have already started the planning process but need professional guidance to bring their vision together and manage the remaining details.

  • Six planning consultations
  • Vendor recommendations for remaining needs
  • Design consultation and implementation
  • Timeline creation
  • Month-of coordination
  • Rehearsal coordination

Month-of Coordination

For couples who have planned their wedding but want to ensure flawless execution on their big day, allowing them to relax and enjoy the celebration.

  • Three planning consultations
  • Vendor confirmation and coordination
  • Detailed timeline creation
  • Rehearsal coordination
  • Full day-of coordination (up to 10 hours)
  • Setup and breakdown supervision

Meet the Founder

Efi Mouratidou

Efi Mouratidou, the heart and soul behind Filoxenia Moments, brings a wealth of experience and a deep-seated passion for creating unforgettable events. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, Efi's meticulous planning and creative flair ensure that every detail is flawlessly executed, leaving a lasting impression on guests and hosts alike.

Meet Our Founder

The creative mind behind Filoxenia Moments

Efi Mouratidou

With a deep passion for creating unforgettable experiences and a background in hospitality management, Efi founded Filoxenia Moments in 2015 after planning her own destination wedding in her family's homeland of Greece.

Drawing on her Greek heritage, Efi named the company "Filoxenia" after the Greek concept of hospitality and generosity shown to travelers – a value she brings to every event she plans.

"Planning a wedding or special event shouldn't just be about organizing details," Efi shares. "It's about creating an atmosphere where every guest feels welcomed, every moment is cherished, and where memories are created that last a lifetime."

With over 10 years of experience in event planning and a network of premium vendors across the country, Efi and her dedicated team of coordinators have helped hundreds of couples and clients bring their dream events to life.

Efi holds a degree in Hospitality Management from Cornell University and is a Certified Wedding Planner with the Wedding Planning Institute. When not creating magical events for her clients, she enjoys traveling with her husband and two children, cooking traditional Greek recipes, and hosting gatherings for friends and family.

The Meaning Behind Our Name

The Greek word "filoxenia" (φιλοξενία) embodies a concept that goes beyond simple hospitality. It represents a sacred duty to welcome and honor guests, to create a warm and generous atmosphere, and to forge connections that transform strangers into friends.

We chose this name because every event we plan is infused with these principles. Whether it's an intimate wedding or a grand corporate gala, we believe in creating moments where people feel genuinely welcomed, where connections are made, and where memories are treasured for years to come.

Just as the ancient Greeks considered it an honor to welcome travelers into their homes, we consider it our greatest privilege to be welcomed into the most significant moments of our clients' lives.

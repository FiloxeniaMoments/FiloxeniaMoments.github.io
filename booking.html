Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our consultation process

What happens during the consultation?

During our 60-minute consultation, we'll discuss your vision, preferences, budget, and any specific requirements for your event. We'll share our approach and how we can help bring your event to life. You'll have the opportunity to ask questions and get to know our team better.

Is the consultation fee refundable?

The $100 consultation fee is non-refundable but will be applied as a credit toward your final package if you choose to book our services within 30 days of the consultation.

How far in advance should I book my consultation?

We recommend booking your consultation at least 9-12 months before your wedding or 6-8 months before other types of events. However, we understand that timelines vary, and we'll do our best to accommodate your schedule.

What should I prepare for the consultation?

It's helpful to have a general idea of your event date, guest count, budget, and any inspiration (such as Pinterest boards or magazine clippings). The more information you can share, the more tailored our advice will be.

What happens after the consultation?

After our meeting, we'll prepare a customized proposal based on your needs and preferences. We'll send this to you within 3-5 business days, and we'll be available to answer any questions you may have about the proposal.
